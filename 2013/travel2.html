Traveling

 Arriving & Departing

Getting to the symposium Hotel:
There will be at least two special buses to transfer participants from Larnaca airport to the hotel on Sunday the 26th of May. Depending on the arrivals of participants this might change to provide a mini bus transfer to the hotel to the maximum number of participants. The exact dates and times of these buses from Larnaca Airport will be announced closer to the date of the meeting on this web page.

The fastest way is via Taxi which can be hired right outside the Airport terminal. The fare is around 65 Euros and it takes up to 30 minutes.
